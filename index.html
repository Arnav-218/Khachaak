About Us

Khachaak is the official photography club of MPSTME, NMIMS. Through our lens, we capture the spirit of campus life—its people, events, stories, and moments.

From phone photographers to DSLR enthusiasts, we bring together a community passionate about visual storytelling, creativity, and exploration.
